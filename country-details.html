Linguistic qualifications

Austrian educational institutions are generally taught in German. If you want to study German, you have to pass the B2 test in German. And you can do this course in Austria if you want. Learning German in government universities will take about £450-500 per semester. But in private institutions that would look a lot more (1500-2500) euros.

IELTS is not essential for admission to university, but IELTS/ MOI score 5. before visa application must be applied. There should be 5. Masters course in English and German. IELTS 6/6. for studying English. 5/ MOI has to be there.

Verification of documents

All academic documents must be verified respectively by educational institutions, Ministry of Education, Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Embassy of Austria. After verifying documents from the ministry when you submit them to the Embassy, Embassy Consulate will send them to the VFS Dhaka office and the fees can cost Rs 43,120 to Rs 47,000. Then after verifying all the documents, VFS will re-send it to the embassy and then verify the embassy consulate.

Admission application process

Austrian universities usually have two semesters. The first one runs from December to February and the next one runs from March to September. Austria Admission, Offer Letter, Document Attestation, Visa – Full Process is time subject (6 to 12 months). So gotta start working early.
List of documents required for admission is given below:
1. Fill out application form
2. Authentic copy of all academic certificates and marksheets
3. IELTS (minimum 6. 0) / German B2 Certificate
4. NID / Passport copy
5 . Motivation letter / CV / Reference letter
6. Work experience / Research paper (if required)

Students in Austria:

Opportunity to study without salary or low cost
Austria is one of the lowest cost countries in Europe to study. Austrian government universities usually don’t have to pay any tuition fees, but the semester fees will be £19. But at the time of admission some money will have to be paid for registration and application fees. But private universities and some courses have to pay tuition fees, but much less than other countries in Europe. Before applying, it is better to know the details about the admission and tuition fees by mailing with the University.
Evidence of Financial Evidence/Bank Solvency Certificate
5897 in your account for Bank Solvency. 16 euros (student if below 24) or 10678. 08 (student if above 24 years) euro. Your sponsor can be any relative from parents-wife. But remember that the source of money, all the documents of tax have to be submitted for visa. Sponsors better if they live in Austria. After getting the visa you can withdraw this money if you want.
